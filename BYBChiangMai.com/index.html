Who We Are

Explore BYB - THE UNDERGROUND Chiang Mai Nightclub,
The Ultimate Subculture Music Experience in the Heart of the City.

Embark on an unforgettable journey of electronic music at BYB Nightclub Chiang Mai, the ultimate after-party destination and haven for underground music enthusiasts. Our vibrant nightclub showcases the best of local and international DJs, spinning an eclectic mix of groovy house music, banging techno, and electrifying tech-house that will keep you dancing all night long.

Our Music Line Art : “Refreshing Electro, Colorful & Groovy Techno-House.
(No Mainstream EDM, No Singalong, No Hardstyle)
Keep On The Underground Vibes and Some Good All Time Classics…”

_Sou aka DJ Swill’O

Nestled in the lively Nimman Soi 17, BYB Nightclub offers an unparalleled experience for electronic music lovers, blending a unique underground atmosphere with with a friendly bar team and cozy spaces for a night to remember. Our state-of-the-art sound system ensures an immersive audio experience that resonates with the pulsating beats from both well-established and up-coming DJs.

Step inside our venue to discover two distinct rooms, each designed to cater to different moods and preferences. Chill out in our cozy lounge, where you can sip on refreshing drinks and unwind with friends, or make your way to the energetic dance floor to let loose and revel in the hypnotic beats of techno and house music.

At BYB, we believe in creating an inclusive and welcoming environment for our guests, where the passion for electronic music unites us all. Stay up-to-date with our latest events and exclusive parties by following us on Facebook and Instagram, and never miss a chance to join the most electrifying nightclub experience in Chiang Mai.

Open from 11pm to 4:20 am, BYB Nightclub is the Ravers & Party People best destination for those seeking an extraordinary night out. Don’t miss out on the electrifying atmosphere and unique musical experience that awaits you at BYB Nightclub Chiang Mai.
